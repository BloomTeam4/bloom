Enough about me, though. Let’s talk about fixing ocean pollution!

Every year, 6.4M tonnes of waste are dumped into the ocean.

Scientists estimate that 12.8 million metric tons of plastic waste are currently floating in our oceans today. That's... pretty gross.

2/3 of the world’s fish are suffering from plastic ingestion.

A plastic bag can kill numerous animals because they take so long to disintegrate. An animal that dies will decompose, releasing the bag for another animal to harmlessly fall victim and once again eat the same bag.

Unfortunately, there are very few regulations that have been put in place to control the amount of waste going into the ocean. Due to this, many aquatic animals must live with the waste in their habitat.

Thankfully, things are finally changing!

It’s much easier nowadays to find products that are plastic-free and eco-friendly. When you go out shopping, you can always check the safety of your item by looking out for this symbol on the packaging.
